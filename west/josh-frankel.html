Joshua D. Frankel, CRPC®

Josh Frankel is vice president and portfolio manager for West Bearing Investments, a division of Ferguson Wellman. Frankel brings a range of professional experiences that enable him to provide a high level of client service and guidance on investment management and planning. He also is tasked with establishing and growing West Bearing in communities that are familiar with the investment style and approach of Ferguson Wellman.

Prior to joining West Bearing, Frankel worked as a financial advisor and retirement planning specialist for Merrill Lynch. He also gained experience in marketing and business development through his work at General Motors R*Works and EJ Gallo Winery after college. He completed his CRPC® Â designation in 2011 and earned his M.B.A. and Bachelor of Science degree from the University of Oregon. While completing his undergraduate degree, he played football for the Ducks under Coach Mike Bellotti.

Frankel currently serves as board president of Greater Portland Hillel, president-elect of Oregon Club of Portland, board member of Order of the O and ambassador board member of New Avenues for Youth.
